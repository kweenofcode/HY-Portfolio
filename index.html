Recommendem

I come from the world of theatre and have a passion for improving representation and diversity, but understand that many artists don't know where to start looking to find collaborators from diverse experiences. Using React and Firebase I built a database for artists from underrepresented communities to self-identify and provide a resource for artists looking to improve the diversity of their practice.
